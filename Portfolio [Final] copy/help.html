Arithmetic Sequences Calculator

tn= t1 + (n - 1) d

The tn means number of terms that you want to find. The t1 refers to the term 1 that the sequence starts with. The 'd' value is the common difference that we get by subtracting t2 - t1.


Geometric Sequences Calculator

tn= t1(r)n-1

The tn means number of terms that you want to find. The t1 refers to the term 1 that the sequence starts with. The 'r' value is the common ratio which we get by dividing the t2 - t1.
